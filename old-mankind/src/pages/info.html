WHAT IS ALL MANKIND AND WHY IS IT STUPID?

All Mankind is Stupid is an art collective that infuses the zany and wildly beautiful art style found in comics, manga, and street art into the world of fashion. Beyond that, the brand seeks to pay tribute to various local artists. On the “extras” section, you can find stories, shitposts, and video collaborations. On the “store page” you can find the latest drops and the most wanted cops. If you have any questions or concerns please submit an inquiry in the "contact page".
